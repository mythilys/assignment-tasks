Ferrero

The Italian MNC founded by Michele Ferrero in the year 1946. It started dealing in India in the year 2004. The brand crafts and produces the ultimate beautiful chocolates that taste well as well as look well. Ferrero Rocher is an international chocolate brand and its chocolates are very famous in India.This company is the third biggest chocolate producer as well as the biggest confectionery company in the world. The brand crafts varieties of splendid chocolates that are awesome in texture and will make you an addict of them. Ferrero was the first brand to offer a collection of premium chocolates in India.

Facts

  • Ferrero Rocher manufactures about 4.6 billion pieces every year throughout the world.
  • The maker of Ferrero Rocher and Nutella is the same man.
  • Pietro Ferrero took almost 5 years to get that flawless round-shape of Ferrero Rocher.
  • Pietro Ferrero Used Hazelnut Instead Of Cocoa.
  • Lecithin, one of its ingredients, treats anxiety, depression and high-cholesterol.
